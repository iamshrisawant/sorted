# Sorted: The Vision Ahead

Sorted is more than just a file organizer—it's the first step toward a local, self-organizing digital memory. While the current version handles technical documents and files, the roadmap focus is on making the system smarter, more intuitive, and proactively helpful.

## Upcoming Milestones

### 1. Scanning Images & PDFs (Local OCR)
*Goal: Understand what's inside scans and screenshots without them ever leaving your computer.*
- **Visual Intelligence**: Adding support for reading text from images and scanned PDFs.
- **Fast Processing**: Ensuring that processing large batches of images doesn't slow down your computer.

### 2. Building a Digital Memory
*Goal: Move from simple "folders" to a connected system that grows with your work.*
- **Relational Memory**: Exploring how extracted details can connect. For example, a receipt from June can be automatically linked to a project proposal from the same time, creating a "memory" that organizes itself.
- **Smart History**: Better tracking of how you organize files so Sorted can learn your personal preferences more accurately.

### 3. Conversational Search & AI Assistant
*Goal: Talk to your files and get answers instantly.*
- **Ask Sorted**: A chat interface where you can ask things like "What did I decide in the meeting last week?" and have Sorted find the exact document and answer.
- **Proactive Assistance**: A long-term goal of building a system-wide assistant that minds your environment. It can provide you with information you might need before you even ask, or automate repetitive tasks with your approval.

---

*Note: This roadmap is a living document. We prioritize privacy, local execution, and a zero-friction user experience as we build these features.*
